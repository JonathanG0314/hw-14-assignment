Spiciest Peppers

Pepper X

3,180,000

Bred by Ed Currie (creator of the Carolina Reaper). Currently recognized by Guinness as the world’s hottest pepper.

Carolina Reaper

2,200,000

Previously the world’s hottest pepper for years; known for its signature wrinkled tail.

Apollo Pepper

2,500,000

A hybrid of Pepper X and Carolina Reaper. Not officially certified, but extremely hot.

Dragon's Breath Pepper

2,480,000

Not officially recognized; originally developed for medical anesthetic applications.

Trinidad Morgua Scorpion Pepper

2,009,000

A naturally occurring pepper from Trinidad; extremely hot with a surprisingly fruity flavor.

7 Douglah Pepper

1,853,000

One of the hottest “chocolate” (brown) peppers.
